About me

More than a hundred thousand years of makeup use have passed, and the definition of beauty continues to change. What began with red pigment clay has evolved into a wonderful world of foundation, rouge, lipstick, blush, mascara, eye shadow, eye liner, and eye caramba!

My works are aesthetically pleasing in every shape and pattern. I'm presenting some of my works here , like Bridal make up, Party look make over and Mehendi.
